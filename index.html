Hellcat Poem.

It is fast as a cheetah.

Roars like a puma.

It is expensive like high class restaurants.

His look is like that of a wild animal.

He has the strength of hundreds of horses.

Hellcats are cars desired by many but achievable by few.

It is a comfortable and attractive car.

It is loud like multiple speakers at high intensity.

It is a 4x4 car because it is all-terrain.

This is the car l want to have by my side to have many adventures.
